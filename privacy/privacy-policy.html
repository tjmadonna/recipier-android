Recipier Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Recipier (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Tyler Madonna ("Developer").

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application collects and stores information on the recipes you save, including but not limited to title and website url. The Application only stores this recipe information on device. This recipe information may leave the device as part of your device backup procedure. Please contact your device manufacturer for more information.

Information on the user of the Application is not collected by the Application.

The information you provide us when you contact us for help will only be used to provide you with support. Any information you provide will be discarded within 7 days of the resolution of the issue.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at dev@tylermadonna.com.
